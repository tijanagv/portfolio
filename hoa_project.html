Landing page for Home Owners Association

landing page design for showcasing CMS modules

Project name: HOA

Role: UI/UX

Timeline: July - August 2022

About Project

The Customer Engine has developed an integrated content management system for residential communities. Innitialy it was used by Ladera Ranch Comunity in USA.

Over a period of five years, they have collected feedback from both staff and residents, concluding that it has improved resident experience as well as staff time management (such as remote reservations, announcements, and schedules).

Project Requirements

The client requested the creation of a landing page for the purpose of showcasing this CMS software. All the modules they offer were to be converted from pdf to web presentation.

In line with the client's brand identity, this landing page should present a clean design and intuitive layout. They also request mockups for every module in their CMS for Home Owners Associations.

I started with brand identity

The objective was to incorporate the logo of the company and the brand color palette into the design of the landing page.

UX Design challenge

The Home Owners CMS Software had 20 different modules, with lots of description of how the module works.

Ui design

Module page

Every module and it's functinoalities are presented in separete pages. Here users can find out more about module benefits and concrete actions he/she can use.
