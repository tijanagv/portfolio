Swing the Beat - Dance school website design

ROLE: Ui design / Coding

Ongoing project

Overview:

Swing the Beat brings new subculture to the local dancing scene. The goal is to represent the values and inform new potential dancers about the culture, history and style on one side and how they can get involved, on the other.

Idea / Concept:

A website with retro look and vintage details with the goal to accentuate the message come join us. The design concept corelates with the joyfullness and playfulness of the dance itself.

Storytelling:

Some sections of this website are made specificly for the purpose of telling a story about history, meaning and aesthetics of the Swing Dance era. Visual elements in form of old fashioned postcards, letters and polaroid are added in addition to that story and website's retro concept.

Color Palette & Font:
