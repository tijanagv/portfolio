The problem of people easily find part-time job, offer their services or find the right person for their specific needs

Of all ages to have job market accsess and gain work experience

Create network of thrustworthy customers

Why?

People use different tools to find occasional job or advertise their services.They are often struggling to find the right sources and they are most likely to relly on recommendation.

business opportunities

This web platform would be perfect tool for people to easily create their network of customers. Bigger community and participation of different companies could add specific bussines value.

Who is part-timer?

Employment statistics in Europe shows that 20% of working population, age 20-64 works part-time

The whole team conducted research based on that premisis.
Using design thinking methodology, we started with interview plan and goals.

Demo 1

Search - Register - Apply

Step 1:
From Home Page new users can easily get all the Info about the platform and browse the Job/Service offers:by keywords by location by categories

Step 2:
Users can view the list of available posts and refine the results by their popularity and date. Short details regarding date, time, price and details are shown on the list. Users can accsess specific job post and apply or contact the owner after Log In or Registration process.

Demo 2

Create Post - Accept - Give reviews

Step 1:
After logging in users have the option to create new job or service offer.

Step 2:
User can track and manage the list of all applicants - accepting and removing them from the list.
After the candidates are accepted, automathic message is sent to their inbox - starting the conversation and further communication.

Step 3:
At the end of the colaboration an email reminder is sent to users for review. It's done by one click, choosing the grade from 1 to 5 stars.
