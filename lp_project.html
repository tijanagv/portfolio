Popsy - Ice cream Landing Page Design

ROLE: Ui design

Fictional Project

Overview:

Popsy Ice Cream is the new local shop aiming to connect and engage with the local community. Their bussiness goal is to promote their products by engaging customers with free goodies and members discount.

Idea / Concept:

The form of the landing page plays the main role in presentation and promoting of a small business. Landing page made of pastel and bright colors as classic representation of a traditional ice cream shop. Bold and dark elements are added to the page to grab visitors attention to the Call to Action button.
