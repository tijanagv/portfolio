About

Dancer - designer - drummer

My design journey started after years of embrasing Vintage aesthetic and exploring styles from Bauhaus and Art Deco to Dadaism and today's Modern Minimalistic look.

I love the history of design and art, how they are shaped by the society and the individuals at the same time.

Dancing to the early Jazz music I stared creating promo material for the local dance scene, making my first steps in Graphic Design. Learning how to code elevated my passion for design and shaped the idea of a dream job.
